CIT 230 DESIGN PRINCIPLES




Design Principle 1: Contrast

Fidelity

fidelity.com

If you look up at the header, there is a great contrast between the green background and white text.






Design Principle 2: Repetition

Investopedia

investopedia.com

The same color scheme is used no matter which page you decide to look at. It has the same header and footer.






Design Principle 3: Fitt's Law

Family Search

familysearch.org

The button to take you to the "create an account" page is big enough that it is quick to click. It doesn't take much time for the user to get to the page. It is easy for big thumbs to use on a small screen
