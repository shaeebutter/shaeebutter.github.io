Purpose of this Site

"How many layers will my kid need to wear today?"

If this is a question you've asked before, you're probably a mom. Sorry dads.. you're just not that thoughtful. I want this site to be for all those moms who just want to take their children outside! I know there are days we get our kids dressed and we're heading out the door when we realize it was all in vain. There's a blizzard outside! I want moms to be able to go to a website where they can check the weather in surrounding areas to see if there is a better place to go nearby.
